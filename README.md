A Froggy Roguelike

**Live. Die. Ribbit.**

*Inspired by Slay the Spire, Balatro, and Inscryption - if you like any of these, you'll probably like FROGGLE!*

**[Play FROGGLE](https://preswes14.github.io/FROGGLE/)**

## About

FROGGLE is a *weirdly empowering*, **delightfully breakable** (**f**)roguelike dungeon crawler featuring:
- An adorable little Tadpole named Tapo who YOU must save, or you will ruin his first birthday!
- 4 Brave Frogventurers (Warrior, Tank, Mage, and Healer) - _can you unlock the secret 5th hero??_
- Rich, unique, tactical turn-based combat. Basic initially; progressively awesomer.
- 10 Unique action sigils with unlimited combo potential
- Swing ANY battle at ANY time with a **lucky D20 roll**!
- Risky encounters, each with a hidden unlockable second stage (can you unlock them all?!)
- Earn and spend **XP** to gain **per-run** upgrades or enhance abilities
- Earn **gold** and spend it between runs to buy **PERMANENT** upgrades for your entire party!
- When you beat the game, the game just started. Clear Standard mode to unlock **FROGGED UP** mode!
- Each combat and encounter has its own custom funky froggy beat!
- Lots of fun tidbits and easter eggs!
